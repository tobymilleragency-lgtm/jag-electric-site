**H1:** Electrical Panel Upgrades

**Lead paragraph:**
Your electrical panel is the hub of your entire home or building. If it's undersized, outdated, or throwing breakers on a regular basis, an upgrade is more than a convenience — it's a safety issue.

**Body copy:**

JAG Electric handles panel upgrades and replacements for homes and businesses throughout the Pittsburg and Galena area. We assess what you have, explain what you need, and do the work right the first time.

**Signs you may need a panel upgrade:**
- Breakers that trip frequently
- A fuse box instead of a circuit breaker panel
- Lights that flicker when appliances run
- Not enough circuits for your current setup
- Adding a new addition, workshop, garage, or EV charger

**What to expect:**
We'll look at your current panel, discuss your power needs, and give you a straight answer on what the upgrade involves. No upsell pressure. Free estimate on every job.

- Sub-panel installation

**H1:** Home Rewiring

**Lead paragraph:**
Older homes were wired for a different era. Knob-and-tube, aluminum wiring, or undersized circuits can create real safety risks — and headaches when you're trying to add circuits for modern living.

**Body copy:**

JAG Electric provides residential rewiring services for older homes, full remodels, and additions. We'll walk you through what's in your walls, what needs to change, and what it takes to do the job properly.

**When rewiring makes sense:**
- Home is more than 40 years old with original wiring
- You're doing a full remodel and walls are already open
- You have known aluminum or knob-and-tube wiring
- Insurance is flagging the wiring
- You want to bring the home up to current code

**What the work involves:**
Rewiring scope varies by project. Timeline depends on the size of the home, what's already in the walls, and how much of the structure is accessible. We'll scope it out honestly before we start.
