## HERO-30: "Live Circuit" (30-second Homepage/Social Cut)

### Concept
The breaker panel comes alive. Copper traces light up one by one across a
dark switchgear surface, then resolve into service icons and a clean CTA.
No voiceover actor needed — music bed + animated captions carry it.
Tone: premium, confident, slightly industrial. Not gimmicky.

### Storyboard

Scene 01 — HOOK (0:00 – 0:04) — 4 sec
  Visual: Black frame. Single copper trace draws itself left-to-right across
  the lower third of frame. At the end of the trace: a small amber indicator
  light pulses ON.
  Caption (animated burn-in, Space Mono): "CIRCUIT OPEN"
  SFX: faint electrical hum, sub-bass pulse on amber light.
  Cut type: hard cut to Scene 02.

Scene 02 — AUTHORITY (0:04 – 0:10) — 6 sec
  Visual: Dark graphite panel. Large headline fades in from opacity-0:
    Line 1 (Inter 800, #F0F4F8): "JAG ELECTRIC"
    Line 2 (Inter 400, cyan glow): "Licensed Electrical Contractor"
  Below: cyan-glow horizontal rule draws itself right.
  Sub-caption row fades in (Space Mono, amber):
    [★ 4.9 Google]  [A+ BBB]  [50 Reviews]
  SFX: single clean relay-click on rule draw.
  No voiceover. Badges are sourced facts; do not invent anything.

Scene 03 — PROOF LOOP (0:10 – 0:18) — 8 sec
  Visual: 3-card horizontal strip, each card on dark panel surface,
  cyan left-border accent, copper top rule.

    Card A (0:10–0:13):
      Icon: circuit breaker SVG outline, cyan fill on activate.
      Label (Space Mono): "PANEL UPGRADES"
      Microtext (Inter 400, muted): "Code-compliant, clean work"

    Card B (0:13–0:16):
      Icon: house outline with circuit trace inside.
      Label: "REWIRING"
      Microtext: "Residential & commercial"

    Card C (0:16–0:18):
      Icon: lightning bolt minimalist outline (copper).
      Label: "OUTLETS · FIXTURES · MORE"
      Microtext: "No job too small"

  Cards animate: slide up from y+20, opacity 0→1, staggered 0.3s.
  Caption rail (bottom center): review pull quote, white on translucent
  dark pill: "The work is high quality!" — Joe's Neighbor, Google Review
  Disclaimer footnote (8pt muted): "Review sourced from Google. Not verified by JAG."

Scene 04 — SERVICE AREA (0:18 – 0:24) — 6 sec
  Visual: Dark map card. Pittsburg KS centered. Concentric cyan ring
  radiates outward ~30-mile radius. City label dots appear one by one:
  Pittsburg, Galena, Frontenac, Girard, Joplin MO.
  Circuit-trace line connects each dot back to Galena (home base).
  Amber pulse on Galena dot.
  Caption burn-in: "Serving SE Kansas & NE Oklahoma"
  Sub-caption: "Pittsburg · Galena · Frontenac · Girard · Joplin area"
  NOTE: map is illustrative of approximate service area; not a claim of
  exclusive territory or precise radius. Do not label as "guaranteed coverage."

Scene 05 — CTA (0:24 – 0:30) — 6 sec
  Visual: Full-bleed graphite panel.
  Headline (Inter 800, cyan glow): "Free Estimates"
  Sub-headline (Inter 400, white): "Call or request online — limited evening & weekend availability"
  Phone number (Space Mono 700, large, amber): "(620) 714-1125"
  URL pill (cyan border): jagelectric.com
  Copper trace draws a border around the CTA block.
  Amber indicator light pulses: "CIRCUIT CLOSED"
  Fade to black at 0:30.

## Hyperframes Composition Notes (for when render is ready)

Project path: /home/toby/jag-electric-site/video-renders/hyperframes/jag-electric/

Init command:
  cd /home/toby/jag-electric-site/video-renders/hyperframes
  npx hyperframes init jag-electric --non-interactive

Recommended template base: blank (custom design, not a stock template)
DESIGN.md: seed from Visual Identity Brief section above.

Per-scene notes:
  - Circuit trace draw: SVG stroke-dashoffset animation via GSAP.
    gsap.fromTo(tracePath, {strokeDashoffset: length}, {strokeDashoffset: 0, duration: 1.5, ease: "power2.inOut"})
    Compute pathLength via path.getTotalLength() at composition load (synchronous).
  - Amber indicator pulse: gsap.to(indicator, {opacity:0, repeat: finite, yoyo:true, duration:0.4})
    Use finite repeat = Math.ceil(duration / 0.8) — never repeat:-1.
  - Card stagger: gsap.from(".service-card", {y:20, opacity:0, stagger:0.3, duration:0.5})
  - Map dot reveal: staggered tl.add() calls on each city-dot element.
  - Font stack: load Inter + Space Mono from Google Fonts in composition <head>.
    Use exact names "Inter" and "Space Mono" — do not use Menlo, SFMono, system-ui.
  - All timelines: paused:true, registered to window.__timelines["jag-hero-30"]
  - No async/await/setTimeout around timeline construction.
  - No repeat:-1 anywhere.

Scene durations for data-composition-id data-duration:
  Scene01: 4s  Scene02: 6s  Scene03: 8s  Scene04: 6s  Scene05: 6s
  Total HERO-30: 30s (transitions: 0.5s crossfade between scenes, baked into durations above)

Transition style: CSS fade-through-black between scenes (NOT shader transitions —
  keep it precise and technical, matching circuit-breaker aesthetic).

Render commands when ready:
  npx hyperframes lint --strict
  npx hyperframes validate
  npx hyperframes inspect
  npx hyperframes render --quality high --fps 30 --output ../../jagelectric-hero30-16x9-v1.mp4
